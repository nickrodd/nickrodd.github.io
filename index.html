Nick Rodd

Miller Fellow at UC Berkeley. Particle phenomenologist interested in dark matter.

My research focusses on indirect detection, which involves searching for the signatures of dark matter in astrophysical datasets. I am also interested in effective field theory, axions, statistics, and collider physics.
