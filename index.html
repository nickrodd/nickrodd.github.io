Nick Rodd

Miller Fellow at UC Berkeley. Particle phenomenologist looking for dark matter.

My research focusses on the search for dark matter in astrophysical datasets, an approach known as indirect detection. I also work on effective field theory, axions, statistics, and collider physics.
