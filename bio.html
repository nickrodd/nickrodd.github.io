Who am I?

Good question. In short, I'm currently a Miller fellow at UC Berkeley, spending half my week in the BCTP and the other half at LBNL. I grew up in Melbourne, Australia, and completed my B.Sc. and LL.B. at Melbourne University. I then completed my M.Sc. in Physics at the same institution, working under the supervision of Ray Volkas and Elisabetta Barberio. Afterwards, I headed to MIT and completed my Ph.D. with Tracy Slatyer focussing on indirect detection.
