Dark Matter

From rotation curves to the cosmic microwave background, the evidence that dark matter constitutes 84% of the matter in the universe is overwhelming. All that remains is to figure out what exactly it is. As a particle phycisist I have the arrogance to believe dark matter must just be a new, as of yet undiscovered, fundamental particle. While it may feel like a leap to posit that measurements made at galactic and cosmological scales demand the existence of a new particle, keep in mind that the atomic hypothesis has proven a successful paradigm for all other forms of matter.
